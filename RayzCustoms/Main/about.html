Ordering Options

Interested? Check out the steps to obtain your pair of custom shoes!

We have 2 available options when it comes to orders:

  • If you own a pair of the shoes listed above, and you would like those customized, contact me and we can figure out a way to get the shoes to me. Pricing for these are anywhere from $20-35, depending on work time, and complexity of design
  • If you do not own of the pair shoes listed above, that's fine! We have an option to buy you your choice of those shoes, customize them, and ship them out to you! Pricing for these include the price of the new shoe, and are between $90-$140
